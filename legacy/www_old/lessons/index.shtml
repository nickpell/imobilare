MIT Imobilare is proud to offer free breakdancing lessons!! (for MIT students) Come learn the fundamentals of breakdancing - no previous experience needed!

WHAT: Breakdancing lessons
DATE: Every Saturday this fall, 3-4 PM at McCormick Dance Room
COST: Free for MIT students and affiliates, $5 for non-MIT students

Class size is limited to 15 students so space is limited! Please sign up in advance by emailing imobilare-exec AT mit.edu

Imobilare has received funding from the MIT Undergraduate Association to offer weekly, one hour breakdancing lessons free of charge to MIT students and affiliates.

These lessons will be taught by Omen of Ground FX Crew and Problems Kru (http://www.gfxcrew.com/). Omen has won numerous breakdancing competitions and was an exhibition performer at Imobilare's School House Rocks 2004 jam. He also has extensive experience teaching breakdancing.
